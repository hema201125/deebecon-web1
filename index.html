Solutions - Off-shore Outsourcing

Many Fortune 500 corporations have already outsourced or in the process of setting their own off-shore development centers(ODC).

Although big corporations may have the capacity and resources to setup their own ODC, still many small and medium size business face a difficult task when it comes to off-shore outsourcing. Our off-shore solutions can help you tackle this problem.

Our off shore development centers can save lot on your development costs. Also to serve your customers better with the advantage of professionals and engineers working in different time zones to provide un-interrupted service. This will also enable you to complete the work faster with more resources in different time-zones.

Off shore outsourcing also guarantees you to get the benefits of host country and its local talents. Our clients either outsource the projects to us to develop, test and support in our facilities or use our services to setup their own off shore development, test and support centers.

A well managed team works with you locally to provide you with day to day status and demos to keep you on updated. This team collects all specifications and works with design phases and co-ordinate the development, testing and support to our off-shore sites.

We also work with various partners in different off-shore countries with their own software development centers.

Our engineers also help manage and administer application servers, ERP applications such as SAP, and increasingly web servers such as MS-IIS, Apache and others.

These services are provided either on-site or off-site based on your needs. We can provide 24x7 support for your systems making sure your applications, tools and everything else run smoothly.
